Aviation Legal Framework

Understand aviation safety rules, licensing requirements, passenger rights, and global aviation compliance standards.

DGCA (India)

DGCA regulates civil aviation safety, pilot licensing, aircraft certification, airline operations, and passenger rights within India.

Passenger Rights – India

Compensation for delays, cancellations, denied boarding, baggage loss, and refunds are mandated under DGCA CARs.

ICAO

International Civil Aviation Organization sets global standards for aviation safety, security, environmental protection, and efficiency.

IATA

IATA represents airlines worldwide and focuses on standardization, safety audits, and operational best practices.
